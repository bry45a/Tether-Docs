---
  Privacy Policy
  Last updated: May 15, 2026
  
  Tether ("we," "our," or "us") built the Tether app to help you stay connected with the people you're closest to. This
  policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

  ---
  Information We Collect

  Information you provide:
  - Phone number — used to create and verify your account. It is never shown to other users.
  - Display name and profile photo — shown to users you are connected with.
  - Photos and posts — content you choose to share with your connections.
  
  Information collected automatically:
  - Push notification token — used to send you notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device
  settings.

  Information from your contacts (optional):
  - If you grant contacts permission, we send phone numbers from your address book to our servers to find people you know
  who are already on Tether. Phone numbers are used only for this matching process and are not stored on our servers.

  ---
  How We Use Your Information
  
  - To create and maintain your account
  - To show your content to people you are connected with
  - To help you find friends already on Tether
  - To send you push notifications about activity relevant to you
  - To process your subscription through Apple's in-app purchase system

  ---
  Sharing Your Information
  
  We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except:
  - Supabase — our backend infrastructure provider, used to store and serve app data
  - RevenueCat — used to manage subscription status; they receive a user identifier but not your phone number or personal
  content
  - Apple — handles all payment processing for Tether+ subscriptions

  ---
  Data Retention and Deletion
  
  You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile,
   photos, posts, connections, and reactions. Some data may remain in backups for up to 30 days before being purged.

  ---
  Children's Privacy
  
  Tether is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  ---
  Changes to This Policy
  
  We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by posting the
  updated policy here.

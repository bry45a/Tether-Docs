Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Tether account and all associated data directly from within the app.

  1. Open the Tether app
  2. Tap the Profile tab
  3. Tap the settings icon in the top right
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete account
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, connections, messages, and photos. This cannot be undone.
